🌐 Ping, Traceroute and DNS: Essential Tools para diagnosticar your network

When your connection drops or a website is unresponsive, these three tools are your best allies. They allow you to understand what's failing and where.

🔎 Ping

Checks if a host is reachable and measures the response time. Ideal for determining whether there's a connection.

🛰️ Traceroute

It shows the path packets take from your computer to a destination. Useful for detecting points of failure or slowness.

📡 DNS Lookup

It allows domain names to be resolved into IP addresses. If it fails, your browser won't know where to go.
